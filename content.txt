Corona Virus Awareness

Even though we are more flexible related to covid-19 let's keep in mind the safety precautions

Masks alone will not protect you but are VERY IMPORTANT not only for your own safety but specially to avoid the spread of ANY virus

Physical distancing is also very important - respect others and if with the virus - stay isolated

Avoid traveling &|| using public transportation if sick &|| protect yourself in public closed spaces

Frequent handwashing is a good practice in any context

Be aware of the medicine approved by the World Health Organization

Be aware to general symptoms but in case of difficulty breathing or shortness of breath, loss of speech or mobility, or confusion, chest pain - go to the hospital for proper treatment

Source: WHO https://www.who.int/
